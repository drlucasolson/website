About

At Plastizyme, we're driven by a single vision: to combat plastic pollution at its source using cutting-edge biotechnology. As pioneers in sustainable plastic degradation, our mission is to empower individuals and communities to reduce plastic waste in a way that is effective and accessible. We believe that every small action contributes to a bigger impact, and with Plastizyme, we can collectively change the future of our planet for the better. Join us in revolutionizing how we manage plastic waste, and together, let’s make a cleaner, greener world.

Waste

Plastic waste has become one of the most pressing environmental issues of our time. Every year, millions of tons of plastic enter landfills and oceans, taking hundreds of years to decompose. Traditional methods of plastic recycling have proven insufficient, with only a small fraction of plastics ever being reused. As plastic accumulates, it causes harm to wildlife, ecosystems, and even human health. The crisis is urgent, and new solutions are needed to address this global challenge. Plastizyme is our answer to reducing the overwhelming burden of plastic waste on our planet.

Product

Plastizyme offers a breakthrough approach to plastic degradation. Our enzyme-based product accelerates the decomposition of PET plastics, breaking them down into harmless components in just days rather than centuries. Unlike traditional recycling, which requires energy-intensive processes, Plastizyme works efficiently and sustainably at a biological level, harnessing the power of nature to tackle plastic waste. Our solution is designed for everyday use, bringing the power of advanced biotechnology into homes, communities, and businesses to make plastic degradation simple and effective.
